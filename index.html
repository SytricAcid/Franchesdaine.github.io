"The Midnight Deadline"

Franchesdaine and Reezca, best friends and chronic procrastinators, ignored their project deadlines until the final night.
Meeting at Franchesdaine’s house, they surrounded themselves with snacks and crumpled notes, determined to finish everything in one night.
As the clock ticked closer to midnight, their fingers flew across the keyboard, fueled by caffeine and panic.

By 12:00 AM, the girls were completely drained, stress and exhaustion taking over.
Just as the clock struck, they both froze, falling silent at their desks.
The next morning, their bodies were found, faces illuminated by their still-glowing screens.
It’s said that ever since, ghostly typing sounds echo from Franchesdaine’s room at midnight, haunting procrastinators everywhere.
